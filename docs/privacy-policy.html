Advertising: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

4. Data Storage

All your learning data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your personal learning data on external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For in-app purchases

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
