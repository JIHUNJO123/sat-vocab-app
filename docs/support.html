Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my progress?

Your learning progress is automatically saved. You can view your statistics including words learned, quiz scores, and study streaks on the home screen and in the Statistics section.

How do I change the translation language?

Go to Settings and select your preferred native language from the available options: English, Korean, Chinese, or Hindi.

How do I remove ads?

You can remove all advertisements by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option in the Settings menu.

Is my data backed up?

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. We recommend not uninstalling the app if you want to keep your progress.

How are difficulty levels determined?

Words are categorized into Basic, Common, Advanced, and Expert levels based on their frequency of use in English. Rarer words are assigned higher difficulty levels.
